rsc-sans.woff2 / rsc-sans-bold.woff2
====================================

These webfonts are a subset (the 95 RSC VALID_CHARS, see
docs/remote-client/specs/font.md §0) of the **Liberation Sans** family,
converted to WOFF2. Liberation Sans is metric-compatible with Helvetica/Arial —
the logical font the authentic RuneScape Classic client rasterizes
(reference/rsc-client/src/client/ui/Panel.java) — so it reproduces the client's
text metrics without an AWT glyph-extraction pipeline.

Source: LiberationSans-Regular.ttf / LiberationSans-Bold.ttf (liberation-fonts).
